Why Blockchain?

Traditional centralized data platforms require blind trust.

  • Escrow Contracts: Guarantee doctors are paid exactly what was promised, exactly when the threshold is met.
  • Soulbound Tokens (SBTs): Create immutable, non-transferable identities, tracking a doctor's lifelong accuracy and reputation score.
  • Regulatory Audit Trails: Every timestamp and annotation hash is stored on Polygon, perfect for FDA 510(k) submissions.

How It Works: The Startup

  1. Uploads raw medical images (X-Rays, Retina Scans).
  2. Platform strictly strips Protected Health Info (PHI).
  3. Company defines labels & deposits USDC into Escrow.
  4. Monitors real-time progress & generates detailed Confusion Matrices.
  5. Receives a mathematically proven, expert-validated dataset.

How It Works: The Doctor

  1. Applies with credentials (Governor approves > Mints SBT).
  2. Connects Wallet or Bank Account.
  3. Reviews assigned batch and annotates via clinical interface.
  4. Cross-referenced with 2+ other doctors for Consensus (IAA metric).
  5. Earns instant USDC payout via Smart Contract release.

Business Model

Monetization designed for massive scale.

  • 10% Protocol Fee: Deducted directly from on-chain escrow upon successful settlement.
  • MAT Token Utility: Pay fees in MAT for a 20% discount. Doctors stake MAT for priority high-paying tasks.
  • Secondary Royalties: 2% perpetual smart-contract royalties to annotators if datasets are resold.
