# Usage Guide

## For AI Companies (Data Requesters)
1. **Connect Wallet:** Use MetaMask to connect to the Polygon network.
2. **Create Dataset Batch:**
   - Go to "Upload Images".
   - Select your image type (e.g., Chest X-Ray) and set your clinical labels.
   - Deposit USDC into the Escrow contract.
3. **Monitor Progress:** Track doctor accuracy and consensus metrics in real-time.
4. **Export Results:** Download the final expert-annotated dataset in JSON format along with a PDF Compliance Report.

## For Medical Specialists (Annotators)
1. **Get Verified:** Apply with your credentials. Once approved by the Admin, you will receive a Soulbound Token (SBT).
2. **Select Task:** Browse available batches in the "Tasks" dashboard.
3. **Annotate with Xai:**
   - Use **Xai Draft** to pre-populate finding boxes.
   - Use **Xai Caption** to generate clinical observations automatically.
   - Refine the work to ensure 100% medical accuracy.
4. **Submit & Earn:** Commit your work to the blockchain. Once the batch is audited, your payment is automatically released to your wallet.

## Example Scenario
A medical startup needs 500 verified X-ray annotations for a new Pneumonia detection model. They create a batch on MediAnnote. Three radiologists independently annotate the same images using Xai tools. The Consensus Engine identifies that two doctors agree on 98% of the findings, while one is an outlier. The two accurate doctors receive a higher reward, and the company gets a verified, high-consensus dataset.
