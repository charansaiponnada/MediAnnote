# Case Study: LungAI Diagnostics

## The Client
**LungAI Diagnostics** is a Series A medical startup developing a deep-learning model for automated Pneumonia detection.

## The Challenge
LungAI needed 10,000 expert-verified annotations for an FDA 510(k) submission. Their previous workflow involved emailing DICOM files to individual radiologists, which resulted in:
1.  **High Costs:** $15.00 per image due to manual coordination.
2.  **Compliance Gaps:** No unified audit trail of who performed the verification.
3.  **Low Velocity:** The project was estimated to take 6 months.

## The MediAnnote Solution
LungAI migrated their dataset to the MediAnnote platform, leveraging our **Startup-Tier** features.

### 1. Xai-Assisted Speed
By using **Xai Smart Drafting**, radiologists were able to start with pre-identified regions of interest. This reduced the average time per image from 4 minutes to 45 seconds.

### 2. Merkle-Proof Audit Trail
MediAnnote generated an on-chain **Provenance Trail** for every single image. LungAI used the **"One-Click Compliance Report"** to provide regulators with cryptographic proof of human-in-the-loop verification.

### 3. Merkle Batching & Gas Efficiency
By batching 100 annotations per Merkle Root, LungAI saved **99.2% in gas fees** compared to traditional on-chain recording methods.

## The Results
-   **Cost Reduction:** Per-image cost dropped from $15.00 to **$4.50**.
-   **Velocity:** The 10,000 image batch was completed in **3 weeks** instead of 6 months.
-   **Regulatory Readiness:** The FDA-Ready Compliance Report was accepted as primary evidence for data integrity during their pre-submission meeting.
