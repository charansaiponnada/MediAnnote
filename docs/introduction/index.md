# Introduction

## Product Overview
**MediAnnote** is a decentralized, Web3-powered medical image annotation platform designed to turn clinical expertise into high-fidelity AI training data. By bridging the gap between medical specialists (Doctors) and AI development companies, MediAnnote ensures that every data point is expert-verified, secure, and provably accurate.

The platform leverages **Xai (Explainable & Assisted AI)** to accelerate the annotation workflow while maintaining a full blockchain audit trail on the **Polygon** network.

## Problem Statement
The development of medical AI is currently bottlenecked by:
1.  **Low-Quality Data:** Most annotation platforms use non-specialist crowd workers, leading to noisy datasets that fail clinical validation.
2.  **Lack of Transparency:** There is no immutable record of who annotated what, or how much the AI influenced the final decision.
3.  **Unfair Compensation:** Specialists are often excluded from the value chain, and cross-border payments for their expertise are slow and expensive.

## Target Users
-   **Medical Specialists (Doctors):** Radiologists, Pathologists, and Dermatologists who want to contribute their expertise to AI development and earn USDC.
-   **AI Research Companies:** Startups and healthcare institutions requiring "Gold Standard" datasets for FDA/CE regulatory submissions.
-   **Regulators & Auditors:** Entities requiring a transparent, blockchain-backed provenance trail for medical AI models.
